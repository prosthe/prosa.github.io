Our Story

2015

Foundation

PROSA was founded by a group of student activists at University of Liberia to address growing concerns about educational inequality.

2015

National Expansion

Expanded to 15 universities across the country, establishing our first national coordinating committee.

2015

Major Campaigns

Successfully led the "Fund Our Future" campaign, securing $50,000 in additional student funding.

2015

Present Day

Now active in 15 universities with over 25,000 members, continuing to fight for student rights and progressive values.

Our Values

Solidarity

We stand together with all marginalized communities and support collective action.

Transparency

We operate openly and are accountable to our members and the communities we serve.

Inclusion

We create spaces where every voice is heard and valued, regardless of background.

Sustainability

We advocate for long-term solutions that benefit both people and the planet.
